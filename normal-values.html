3DKA (glucose + ketones)Glucose, VBG, ketones, K⁺Fluids → check K⁺ → fixed-rate insulin. Check K⁺ before insulin.
4Anaemia (low Hb)FBC, MCV, reticulocytesHb <70 = transfusion threshold. Check MCV for type.

6HyponatraemiaSodium, osmolality, fluid statusSlow correction — rapid correction causes osmotic demyelination

Paediatric Red Flags:
Temperature >38°C in infant <3 months = EMERGENCY — escalate immediately
Capillary refill >3 seconds = concerning — assess circulation
Neonatal glucose <2.6 mmol/L = hypoglycaemia — treat immediately
Non-blanching rash + fever = meningococcal disease until proven otherwise
