🌟 Universal Opening Script — every examination
Hello, my name is [name], one of the doctors. Can I confirm your name and date of birth? With your permission, I'd like to examine your [area]. I'll explain everything as I go, and you can ask me to stop at any time. Before I start — is there any area that is already painful?
✓ Universal Closing Script
Thank you — that's the end of the examination. I'll help you cover up. I'll clean my hands again. Summary: [positive findings] / [negative findings] / [interpretation] / [next steps].
⛔ Universal Instant Fails — Any Examination
⚠ Silent examination — no verbalisation means no marks
⚠ No explicit consent before touching patient
⚠ No chaperone offered for intimate examination
⚠ No hand hygiene
⚠ Forgetting to compare both sides
⚠ Examining through clothing
