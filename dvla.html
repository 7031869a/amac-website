For safety reasons you should avoid driving for the recommended period. It is your legal responsibility to inform the DVLA, and I will document that we discussed this today.

🩸 Hypoglycaemia While Driving — Immediate Actions
1
Stop the vehicle as soon as it is safe to do so.
2
Remove keys from the ignition — commonly missed in OSCE.
3
Move to the passenger seat — also commonly missed.
4
Take fast-acting carbohydrates (glucose gel, sugary drink).
5
Do NOT resume driving until glucose is normal AND symptoms fully resolved.
