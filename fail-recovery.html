Fail Recovery Diagnostic

Answer 15 questions about what happened in your exam. The tool calculates your failure profile and generates a targeted day-by-day repair plan.

Key insight
For many candidates, the problem is not lack of knowledge but difficulty translating knowledge into safe, structured performance under exam pressure. This diagnostic identifies what actually caused your failure — not what you think caused it.
How this works
1
Answer 15 questions about what actually happened in your exam — behaviours, not diagnoses.
2
The tool calculates your failure profile across 9 domains with a percentage score for each.
3
You get a primary repair target and a personalised 5-day recovery plan with exact chapters, stations and drills.
4
Drill that domain for 3–5 days before returning to full revision.
