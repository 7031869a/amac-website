The fluency trainer

Speak Like a Doctor

You can know the medicine and still lose marks because of how it sounds. This trainer fixes the words, the warmth, and the timing the examiner is quietly scoring — across the eight moments where a consultation is won or lost.

This is not accent reduction, and it never asks you to sound like anyone but yourself. The promise is simpler: phrase it the way that scores — natural, warm, and in the register a UK examiner is listening for.
